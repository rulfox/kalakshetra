Generic white surface card — soft warm shadow, near-square corners. Use for any boxed content; for products use `ProductCard`.

```jsx
<Card hoverLift>
  <h3>How to Order</h3>
  <p>Tap → we confirm size, fabric & price → we hand-paint & ship.</p>
</Card>
```

`padded={false}` when the card wraps full-bleed media. `hoverLift` for interactive cards.
