Recurring section header: gold eyebrow + short rule, serif title, optional intro.

```jsx
<SectionHeading eyebrow="The Philosophy" title="Ayurvedic roots, painted by hand"
  intro="Every piece begins on a blank weave and a quiet morning in Mavelikkara." />
<SectionHeading align="center" tone="light" eyebrow="Lookbook" title="Worn, not just made" />
```

`align="center"` for centered intros; `tone="light"` on dark sections.
