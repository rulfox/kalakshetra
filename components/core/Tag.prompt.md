Small uppercase pill tag for product categories (Men / Women / Kids), themes, and quiet status labels.

```jsx
<Tag tone="gold">Men</Tag>
<Tag tone="green">Kathakali</Tag>
<Tag tone="wood">Made to order</Tag>
```

Tones: `gold` (default) | `wood` | `red` | `green` | `indigo`. Mural-accent tones (red/green/indigo) should stay rare per the <10% accent rule.
